Claims & underwriting

Tag: Product, Claim, underwriting

Underwriting is a process that an insurer assesses/categorize whether to accept a policy, through request of personal information. Additional term may apply.

 

 

Purpose

Assess the risk acceptance to avoid anti-selection and lapse risk

Categorize the risk by risk rating applying appropriate premium, apply loading or decline the risk

Ensure the risk is within the risk appetite from an insurance prespective

Cost

Medical assessment, professional underwriter

For loss and complicated underwriting, it will be unattractive for the customer

There is a trade-off between it. Technology may help

Actuaries

Assist/advise to build up the model, e.g. classification problems and get the right factors.
