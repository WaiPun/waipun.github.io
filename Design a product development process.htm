Cyclical process

Systematic way to identify needs and fulfil with a suitable product: innovate, create and review products.

Actuary roles

In every part. Note that actuaries tend to be out of touch with the market. Hence, unlike the valuation, inputs from other team (distributions, agency, legal are very important).

·         Key part: experience investigation and profit testing (pricing), risk, capital.

·         Input: advise on risk, benefits design, underwriting, claim and administration. Holistically, capital requirement, investment policy and legislation.
